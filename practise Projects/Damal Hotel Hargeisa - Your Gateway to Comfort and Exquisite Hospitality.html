Contact Us

What is the checkout time?

Check out time is 12:00 noon, should you wish to extend your departure time or date, kindly contact our front desk/reception at least a day before your scheduled date or time of departure to verify room avaliability.

Worried about your valuable items like gold or jewelry?

Damal Hotel will not assume responsibility for any undeclared valuables like (Jewelry or Money) lost in the room. During check in you may deposit your valuables with the front desk/reception for their security.

How should I behave during my stay?

Loud conversation, Music, Tv and unnecessary noise in the guest rooms should be avoided so as not to disturb other guests.

What about Damage/loss of Hotel Property?

Any damage made to any property of the hotel either by the guest or your visitor, will be billed according to your guest folio. Any loss of hotel’s property like linens, towels, ashtrays, etc, will also be billed to the guest folio, Should you wish to acquire any of our items for souvenir purposes kindlly visit souvenir and boutique shop for better selecting.

Shall I bring Pets or Alchool inside the hotel?

Animals or any kind of animals are not allowed in the hotel rooms.
No Alcoholic drink is being served in any of our bars, coffee shops and restaurants. like wise, we STRICTLY don’t allow the taking and/or drinking of alcoholic drinks inside the rooms and/or within the hotel premises.
